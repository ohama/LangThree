## Issues Found and Resolved During Audit

### PipeRight + BuiltinValue (FIXED)

**Issue:** `|>` operator only handled `FunctionValue` on the right side, not `BuiltinValue`. This caused `"hello" |> string_length` to fail at runtime even though type checking passed.

**Fix:** Added `| BuiltinValue fn -> fn argVal` case to `PipeRight` handler in Eval.fs.

**Test:** Updated `pipe-with-string.flt` to test direct piping (removed wrapper workaround), added `pipe-builtin-print.flt`.
